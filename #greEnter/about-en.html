What is #greEnter?

#greEnter is not just a project, it is a true movement towards a green future for Moldova, starting with its youngest citizens! Imagine a team of superheroes, but instead of capes, they have composters, and instead of superpowers – knowledge about ecology. #greEnter aims to turn schools into centers of ecological awareness, where every student becomes an agent of change.

Who are we?

We are a diverse team, including local residents, staff, volunteers, partners, dreamers, and innovators, who contribute to the development and activities of #greEnter.
